Introduction

At URL Player, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

In short: We do not collect any personal data, and we do not share your information with any third parties. All your data is stored locally on your device only.

Information We Collect

1. Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Video Links: The URLs you add for videos you want to watch
  • Link Titles: The names you give to saved links
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as theme (light/dark) and language
Important: This data is stored only on your device using a local database (ObjectBox). It is not sent to any server or shared with any party.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect any of the following information:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device information or device identifiers
  • Browsing history or viewing history
  • Any analytics or statistical data

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used only for:

  • Displaying your saved links: To provide quick access to your favorite videos
  • Search: To search through your saved links easily
  • Saving preferences: To remember your settings (theme, language)

Information Sharing

We do not share any information with any third parties whatsoever.

All your data is stored locally on your device and is not transferred or shared with:

  • Our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Advertising companies
  • Analytics companies
  • Any other third parties

Security

We are committed to protecting your data through:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored on your device only
  • HTTPS Only: The app uses secure connections (HTTPS) for streaming videos
  • No Accounts: The app does not require account creation or login
  • Secure Local Database: We use ObjectBox to store data securely

Required Permissions

The app requests only the following permission:

  • Internet (INTERNET): To stream videos from the URLs you add

Note: We do not request any other permissions such as camera, microphone, location, or contacts access.

Your Rights

You have the full right to:

  • Delete Data: You can delete any saved link at any time from within the app
  • Uninstall the App: When you uninstall the app, all stored data is permanently deleted
  • Full Control: You have complete control over your stored data

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services for data collection or analytics. However:

  • When playing a video from a specific URL, the privacy policy of that video's website may apply
  • We do not control the privacy policies of external websites

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes through:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • In-app notification (in case of material changes)

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically.
